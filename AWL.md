# Academic Word List (AWL)

The **Academic Word List (AWL)** is a list of 570 word families that appear most frequently in academic texts across a wide range of disciplines. Using these words can help improve the precision and professionalism of your research writing.

## üí° Writing Tips
- **Precision**: Use these words to be exact. Instead of "looking at," use "analysis."
- **Conciseness**: Use "desiderata" instead of "things we want."
- **Variety**: Use synonyms from this list to avoid repetition.
