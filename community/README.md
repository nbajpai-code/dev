### In-Person Networking

#### At Conferences
- ‚úÖ Prepare elevator pitch
- ‚úÖ Bring business cards or digital contact
- ‚úÖ Attend social events and after-parties
- ‚úÖ Ask thoughtful questions during sessions
- ‚úÖ Follow up within 48 hours

#### At Meetups
- ‚úÖ Arrive early
- ‚úÖ Introduce yourself to organizers
- ‚úÖ Participate in discussions
- ‚úÖ Exchange contact with 2-3 people
- ‚úÖ Attend regularly to build relationships

### Building Your Network

#### Quality Over Quantity
- Focus on meaningful connections
- Provide value to others
- Be genuine and authentic
- Follow up and stay in touch

#### Give Before You Ask
- Help others with their questions
- Share useful resources
- Make introductions
- Contribute to discussions

## üéØ Networking Goals

### Short-term (1-3 months)
- Join 2-3 online communities
- Attend 2-4 local meetups
- Connect with 10-15 people on LinkedIn
- Contribute to 1 open source project

### Medium-term (3-6 months)
- Become active in 1-2 communities
- Speak at a local meetup
- Build relationships with 5-10 key contacts
- Regular open source contributions

### Long-term (6-12 months)
- Organize or co-organize a meetup
- Speak at a conference
- Mentor others in the community
- Build a strong professional network
