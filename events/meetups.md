## Regular Event Types

### Code & Coffee
**Format**: Casual coding sessions over coffee
**Frequency**: Weekly or bi-weekly
**Best For**: Networking, working on side projects, getting help
**Find**: Search "Code and Coffee" + your city on Meetup.com

### Hack Nights
**Format**: Collaborative coding on open source or civic projects
**Frequency**: Weekly or monthly
**Best For**: Contributing to real projects, learning by doing
**Find**: Code for America brigades, local hackerspaces

### Lightning Talks
**Format**: Short 5-10 minute presentations
**Frequency**: Monthly
**Best For**: Sharing quick insights, practicing public speaking
**Find**: Local tech meetups often feature lightning talk nights

### Study Groups
**Format**: Group learning sessions for courses or technologies
**Frequency**: Weekly
**Best For**: Structured learning, accountability
**Find**: freeCodeCamp, Coursera study groups

### Suggested Monthly Cadence
- **Week 1**: Language-specific meetup (JavaScript, Python, etc.)
- **Week 2**: Technology meetup (AWS, Kubernetes, etc.)
- **Week 3**: General networking or diversity event
- **Week 4**: Hack night or study group

- Most meetups are 6-9 PM on weekdays
- Weekend hackathons and workshops
- Virtual options for flexibility
- Aim for 2-4 meetups per month
